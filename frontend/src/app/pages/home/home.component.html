What is CDSS?

A Clinical Decision Support System (CDSS) assists healthcare professionals in evaluating patient information and supporting clinical decision-making.

This platform combines machine learning models and expert clinical rules to provide cardiovascular risk assessment, patient monitoring and clinical alerts.

  • Cardiovascular Risk Prediction
  • Ensemble Machine Learning
  • Expert Clinical Rules
  • Patient History Tracking
  • JWT Authentication
  • Clinical Alerts
